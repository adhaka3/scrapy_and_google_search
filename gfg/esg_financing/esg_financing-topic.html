ESG & Sustainable Finance

The environmental, social and governance (ESG) is shifting towards investment strategies that revolve around green finance. What role does ESG have in emerging sustainable investment funds and what does it mean for the future of financial markets? Learn about market and political sentiment that is influencing the growth of sustainable investment opportunities worldwide with our extensive and in depth advanced analytics.

Growing concerns about the environment has led to major developments in green, sustainable technology and energy sources. With that, investment strategies are also shifting with environmental finance at the forefront of everchanging market trends. 

Stay abreast of how different regions and industries are adapting to new, progressive ESG mandates, centered around topics such as governance, transparency, environmental, investor and political sentiment.
